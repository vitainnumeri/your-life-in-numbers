How it works

Your Life in Numbers turns your date of birth into a live portrait of everything your body has quietly been doing since the day you were born. The moment you enter your details, your browser calculates — in real time, right in front of you — how many seconds you have been alive, how many times your heart has beaten, how many breaths you have taken and much more.

Every number is an estimate based on well-established scientific averages. A resting heart, for instance, beats faster in babies (around 130 beats per minute) and settles to roughly 72 beats per minute in adults, while we breathe about 16 times a minute and blink around 15 times a minute while we are awake. We apply these averages across each stage of your life to reach your personal totals.

Did you know? 🤯

  • ❤️ By the age of 30, your heart has already beaten more than 1 billion times — without ever taking a break.
  • 🫁 You take around 20,000 breaths every single day, almost all of them without ever thinking about it.
  • 👁️ You blink so often that your eyes are closed for roughly 10% of your waking life.
  • 🩸 In a single day, your heart pumps about 7,000 liters of blood around your body.
  • 🌍 Right now you are racing around the Sun at about 107,000 km/h — and you cannot even feel it.
  • 😴 By the age of 75, you will have spent roughly 25 years asleep.

Questions & Answers

Is my data saved anywhere?
No. Everything is calculated locally in your browser. We never store or send your name or date of birth.

How accurate are the numbers?
They are realistic estimates based on scientific averages, made for fun and curiosity — not a medical measurement of your own body.

Is it really free?
Yes, completely free. The site is supported by the ads shown around the page.
