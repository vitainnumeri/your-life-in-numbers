Privacy & Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Your personal data

This site calculates your life statistics directly in your browser. The data you enter (first name, last name, date of birth, height, weight) is never saved on our servers nor sent to third parties: it stays on your device and is used only for the calculation.

If you share your result via the link, your name and date of birth are included in the link address that you choose to share.

2. Cookies and advertising

To cover its costs, this site shows advertisements through Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies to show ads based on previous visits to this and other websites.

  • Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads.
  • The use of advertising cookies enables Google and its partners to show ads to users.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising from Google's Ads Settings.

3. Usage statistics

We may use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about visits (number of visitors, page views), solely to improve the site.

4. Your rights

Since we do not retain personally identifiable data, there is nothing to request or delete from our records. To manage cookies you can use your browser settings.

5. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach the site administrator through the contact options made available on the site.
